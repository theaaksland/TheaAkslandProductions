About Thea Aksland

Thea Aksland is a singer, songwriter, pianist, and producer whose music has captured the hearts of fans around the world. As a solo artist, she has been taking the industry by storm, creating music that resonates with listeners from all walks of life. With her unique sound and soulful lyrics, Thea has become an inspiration and a role model to many. With a relentless drive to create, Thea Aksland has been continuously writing and recording new singles, showcasing her creativity and passion for music. Since her emergence on the scene in 2020, she has been earning the admiration and affection of fans worldwide, who look up to her as both an inspiration and a role model. Beyond her success as a musician, Thea is also committed to breaking down barriers and inspiring future generations of artists. With her unwavering dedication to her craft, she is changing the face of music and paving the way for others to follow in her footsteps. As a pianist and producer, Thea brings a unique perspective to her music, creating rich, layered soundscapes that are both captivating and emotional. With each new release, she continues to push the boundaries of what is possible in music, cementing her place as one of the most exciting and innovative artists of her generation.

Leave Me The Fuck Alone

An ode to self reflection. I spent a year writing and recording this album and diving deep into my inner self. Life isn’t pretty and I surely am not perfect. From feeling like a burden in every situation to falling in love, 2022 was full of surprises.

Love Line

Love line is all about love. The first album I ever released and the most influential to my music journey. From your typical love stories and feeling of betrayal, Love line digs deeper into concepts like falling in love with a serial killer and being in love during the 60s.

Singles

Christmas With Snow

Christmas with snow came from my longing for home during the winter time in New Orleans. Growing up in Colorado, snowfall was a key part in the holiday season and I spent this christmas alone and in the city of New Orleans. Longing for the Christmas spirit, I wrote this song to capture a memory.

Photographs

Dedicated to Melanie and David Aksland, my parents. Growing up, my parents have been my inspiration and example of love. They continually retell old stories about their relationship and the moments they savor even forty years down the road. I wanted to write them something special and write about true love.

Face My Truth

Good old high school heartbreak. Face my truth is about the toxic feelings of love, when you know it’s bad for you but that only makes you want it more. Acknowledge your denial and fall deeper in love with the untouchable.
